Why this exists: Every free Sudoku site on the internet apparently needs 12 banner ads, an autoplay video, and a pop-up asking you to subscribe to a newsletter. You don’t want any of that. Nobody does.

So I built this — clean, ad-free Sudoku. No accounts, no tracking, no cookie banners. Just the 9×9 grid and your brain. Vibe-coded with Claude in an afternoon because I was genuinely annoyed.

Before business school, I spent four years in P&C insurance working across actuarial analytics, pricing, and portfolio strategy at AXA XL, Bajaj Allianz, and WTW — turning messy data into real business decisions.

These days I study Strategy, Finance & Marketing at Vanderbilt Owen by day, and tinker with Python, SQL, and whatever new tool makes building things faster by night. This app is one of those experiments.
